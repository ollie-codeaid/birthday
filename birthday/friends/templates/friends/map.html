The Plan

One day, five pubs, lots of fun!

We will be kicking off at 3.30pm at The Grapes and meandering towards the Dickens Inn throughout the afternoon/early evening (see route detail below). I will be setting challenges over the course of the day and awarding points accordingly - challenges probably won't be rinks based. Points will be recorded live on this website. As the competition will undoubtebly be fierce I will offer the invaluable prize of a handwritten letter of my gratitude to the winner.

If you can't join from the start then feel free to catch us up along the way. I'll be doing my best to keep to the timetable at the bottom of this page.
